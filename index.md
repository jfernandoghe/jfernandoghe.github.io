Tutor: _Carlos Lara-Alvarez_ – [carlos.lara@cimat.mx](mailto:carlos.lara@cimat.mx)

-**National Polytechnic Institute (IPN)** _2010-2015_ (Robotics & intelligent systems) – BSc Mechatronics<br/>
